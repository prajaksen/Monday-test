CircleCI job idea for the PoC

1. Trigger the job from Monday automation or a manual webhook.
2. The job checks out this repository.
3. The job sets the required environment variables:
   - POSTGRES_PASSWORD
   - NAMESPACE
   - POSTGRES_POD
   - POSTGRES_DB
   - POSTGRES_USER
   - MONDAY_TENANT_NAME
   - MONDAY_USER_EMAIL
   - MONDAY_ITEM_ID
   - MONDAY_BOARD_ID
   - MONDAY_STATUS
4. The job runs the SQL update script:
   bash scripts/update-langfuse.sh
5. The job can optionally post the output to a Slack or Monday update channel.
